Vocalist for Pegboy, and the Bhopal Stiffs before that.

Guitarist from Swedish band Souls (together with Cecilia Nordlund). Steve recorded their album "tjitchischtsiy (sudêk)" in Sweden.
Mark Davies
Vocalist, guitar, optigan and banjo player in Thinking Fellers Union Local 282.
Kurt Davis
Dominique Dawson
Cellist; played cello on A Minor Forest's first LP Flemish Altruism Constituent Parts 1993-96. AMF broke up. She's now in Threnody Ensemble (Link?) with ex-AMF guitar player Erik Hoverstein.
Kelley Deal
Sister of Kim Deal. Guitarist for the Breeders and Kelley Deal 6000.
Kim Deal
Bassist for the Pixies, the Breeders and the Amps.
Troy Deckert
Peter Deimel
Sound engineer at Black Box Studio, owned by Iain Burgess. Has worked with Chokebore, Uzeda and others.

Patty DePuye
Jean-Pierre Derosiers
Reid Diamond

Myra Dickerson
Elliot Dicks
Did Post-production mixes for audio, for the Chicago-music documentary "Out Of The Loop". Has produced records for Rainer Maria (Link?), among others. He owns all of the sound equipment at the Fireside Bowl (Link?), and often does sound at shows. AND he drums for The Nerves (Link?).
Matt Diehl
Rolling Stone writer who used to be in Nadsat Rebel (Link?), who were on WNUR's Middle Of America compilation.
Zak Dieringer
Bassist for Craw.
Jochen Distelmeyer

Jeff Ditzenberger
Guitarist of Milwaukee band Powerwagon (Link?), as well as secondary guitar in Killdozer's final lineup. Can be heard on The Last Waltz live album.
Bill Dolan
Chicago area artist. http://members.aol.com/billdolan/
Michael Dolan
Stan Doty
Owner of Pravda Sound, live sound contractor at Lounge Ax and Empty Bottle (Link?).
Tad Doyle
Vocalist for Tad, and has also done some work with Mark Lanegan.
Rebecca Dudley
Was drummer in M.O.T.O., and wife of John Mohr of Tar.
Nance Duffy
Greg Dunlap
Member of Buzzmuscle and Luckyj (Link?), current member of Municipal (Link?). Former editor of THIS fanzine.
Marco Dupont
Santiago Durango
Early guitarist for Naked Raygun through Basement Screams. Later, became guitarist for Big Black. Was in Arsenal (Link?). He's married to Cath Carroll (Link?), and has represented Cynthia Plaster Caster legally. Also represented Corey Rusk & Touch and Go in the lawsuit with the Butthole Surfers (Link?).
A.J. Dusek
Touch and Go Racing Team member
David DuVal
Former accountant at Electrical Audio, singer in Ms Fits cover bands that opened for Shellac Sex Pistols show.
Lili Dwight
Wife of Byron Coley.
