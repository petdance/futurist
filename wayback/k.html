Greg Kurczewski
Direct from Greg:
I own a guitar/drum shop called Rockhaus in Milwaukee and have on occasion supplied Steve with equipment. I also drove the Shellac van and did sound in 1995 on a tour with Shellac, Uzeda, and Dis. I get a lot of questions about Steve's trademark pedal, the Interfax "Harmonic Percolator". They were built in Milwaukee in the 70's. Steve got his from my buddy Jay Tiller of Couch Flambeau (Link?). If anyone is interested, I know a guy who makes exact replicas of the Percolator. Here's his website: http://pages.prodigy.net/chuckcollins/percolator.html
