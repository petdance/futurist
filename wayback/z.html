Tom Zaluckyj
Ex-bassist for Tar. Also ex of Luckyj (Link?). Was employed by Electrical Audio (Steve's studio) for an extended period in the mid 90s, both before and after the move to the new building. Played a fairly major role in the construction of the studio. Engineered many recordings there as well.
